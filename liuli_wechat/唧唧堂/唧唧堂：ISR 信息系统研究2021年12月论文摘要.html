1. 基于图的蚁群算法用于解决组合拍卖中的赢家确定问题


组合拍卖（CA）是指在潜在竞标者之间分配捆绑物品的一种拍卖方式。然而，为防止投标人的偏好诱导，组合拍卖以迭代的方式进行。中标者的确定是限制此类迭代式组合拍卖广泛采用的一个关键瓶颈。由于每轮报价的多样性和速度有所增加，在有时间限制的情况下确定中标者变得更加复杂，而常规的求解器，如IBM CPLEX和A Mathematical Programming Language，在短时间内确定中标者方面显然是无效的。因此，基于启发式的方法具备了更多的适用性。本研究提出了一种基于蚁群的任意时间算法（colony–based anytime algorithm or traveling ants for combinatorial auctions, TrACA），可以在规定时间内产生最优或接近最优的确定中标者的结果，从而丰富了基于启发式的中标者确定方面的工作。本研究在94个公开测试实例上进行了实验，这些实例显示了不同的出价组合。本研究展示并比较了TrACA与20种最新的启发式算法和两种最好的精确算法（CPLEX和Max W Clique）的性能。与20个启发式算法中的12个采用经典搜索（如随机局部搜索、塔布搜索）或遗传或记忆算法相比，TrACA在所有实例上的表现都明显更好。在其他8个采用先进的混合搜索方法的启发式算法（例如，超启发式算法，有偏见的随机密钥）中，TrACA在更难的测试实例上表现得更好。此外，本研究提出的TrACA解决了时间和准确性之间的权衡问题，在94个实例中的76个实例上实现了最优解，所需时间最多为精确算法的六分之一。最后，本研究分析了TrACA的搜索过程，并强调了影响TrACA适合在预先指定的（短）时间内解决困难的拍卖实例的因素。


Combinatorial auctions (CAs) are used to allocate bundles of items among interested bidders. However, to resolve bidder preference elicitation problems, CAs are conducted iteratively. Winner determination is a key bottleneck that restricted the widespread adoption of such iterative CAs. Time bounded winner determination is further complicated by the increased variety and velocity of bids each round, and so regular solvers such as IBM CPLEX and A Mathematical Programming Language have been demonstrably ineffective in determining winners within a short duration. In response, heuristic-based methods have enjoyed an increase in applicability. We add to the growing body of work in heuristic-based winner determination by proposing an ant colony–based anytime algorithm (traveling ants for combinatorial auctions, or TrACA) that produces optimal or near-optimal winner determination results within specified time. Experiments are performed on 94 open test instances that display a variety in bid-bundle composition. We show and compare the performance of TrACA to 20 most recent state-of-the-art heuristics and two of the best exact algorithms (CPLEX and Max W Clique). Compared with 12 of 20 heuristics that employ classic search (e.g., stochastic local search, tabu search) or genetic or memetic algorithms, TrACA does significantly better on all instances. On the other eight heuristics that employ advanced hybrid search methodologies (e.g., hyperheuristics, biased random keys), TrACA performs significantly better on the harder test instances. Additionally, we show that TrACA resolves the trade-off between time and accuracy by achieving optimal solutions on 76 out of 94 instances, in at most one-sixth the time taken by exact algorithms. Finally, we analyze the TrACA search process and highlight factors that make TrACA suitable for solving hard auction instances within a prespecified (short) duration.


论文原文：

Abhishek Ray, Mario Ventresca, Karthik Kannan (2021) A Graph-Based Ant Algorithm for the Winner Determination Problem in Combinatorial Auctions. Information Systems Research 32(4):1099-1114.



2. 看看运费: 基于会员制的免费送货方案对电子零售商的经济影响


本研究探讨了基于会员制的免运费（membership-based free-shipping, MFS）方案的经济影响，该方案是对广泛接受的条件免运费（contingent free-shipping, CFS）政策的一种补充。在CFS方案中，如果订单超过一定的门槛，消费者就可以免除运费附加费，而在MFS方案中，消费者需要预付一笔费用，并在整个会员期享受免运费。本研究建立了一个程式化模型，从以下两个方面考虑消费者的异质性：（1)购买没有实用性的辅助产品（消费者在其他情况下不会购买的产品），以获得CFS的资格；（2）在一定时期内的购物频率。本研究表明，引入MFS后，网络零售商可以进一步细分市场，这可能导致更高的产品价格。当购买用处不太大的辅助产品时，网络零售商应只针对放弃CFS的频繁购物者收取高额的会员费。然而，网络零售商应该降低会员费，以吸引（1)在不实用性较高的情况下，本来就会购买CFS的经常购物者；或（2）在不实用性较低的情况下，本来会放弃CFS的不经常购物者。在所有情况下，尽管收取的费用收入永远无法补偿提供免费送货服务所产生的成本，但作为一个更细分的市场的理想结果，损失可以得到补偿。本研究对网络零售商运输政策的优化设计提出了见解，揭示了网络零售商所面临的实践挑战。


This research examines the economic impact of membership-based free-shipping (MFS) programs when introduced as an augmentation to widely accepted contingent free-shipping (CFS) policies. Under CFS, consumers waive the shipping surcharge if the order exceeds a certain threshold, whereas under MFS, consumers pay an upfront fee and enjoy the free-shipping perk throughout the membership period. We develop a stylized model that considers consumer heterogeneity in two dimensions: (1) disutility from acquiring an auxiliary product—a product that consumers would not otherwise purchase—to qualify for CFS; and (2) shopping frequency over a given period. Our analysis suggests that the introduction of MFS allows the e-tailer to segment the market further, which could lead to a higher product price. When the disutility from acquiring an auxiliary product is moderate, the e-tailer should charge a high membership fee to target only frequent shoppers who forgo CFS. However, the e-tailer should lower the fee to lure also (1) frequent shoppers who would otherwise take CFS, when the disutility is high; or (2) infrequent shoppers who would otherwise forgo CFS, when the disutility is low. In all cases, although the collected fee revenue can never compensate for the cost arising from offering the free-shipping perk, the loss can be recouped as a desirable outcome of a more segmented market. Our research generates insights into the optimal design of e-tailers’ shipping policies, shedding light on the practical challenges that confront e-tailers.


论文原文：

Zhen Fang, Yi-Chun (Chad) Ho, Xue (Jane) Tan, Yong Tan (2021) Show Me the Money: The Economic Impact of Membership-Based Free Shipping Programs on E-Tailers. Information Systems Research 32(4):1115-1127.

https://doi.org/10.1287/isre.2021.1001



3. 利用大规模的社交媒体数据衡量品牌好感度


近几十年来，品牌管理者通过调查消费者来监测品牌的健康状态并改进调查以解决与抽样偏差、回应偏差、引导性等相关问题。然而，随着Web2.0和互联网发展，消费者转向社交媒体来表达对各种话题的看法，并随之产生了大量与品牌互动的数据。研究者特别关注分析这些公开数据来衡量品牌的健康状态。本文的重点是开发一种测量品牌好感度的方法，同时考虑社交媒体发布者所表现出的测量偏差。具体来说，本文提出基于概率图模型的集体推理框架，并采用基于区块的马尔科夫链蒙特卡洛抽样技术，获得与传统测量品牌使用的方法相关的一种调整后的品牌好感度衡量方法。本文在Facebook上收集了超过3300个品牌数据，通过Facebook品牌页面与品牌互动的用户大约有2.05亿。本研究采用的庞大的数据集中包含了66.8亿个赞和10.1亿个用户评论，为建模工作带来了挑战。通过样本外预测、外部真实测试和模拟对研究模型的有效性进行评估。分析结果表明，本文提出的研究模型表现优异，为品牌管理者提供了一种利用社交媒体数据更准确地衡量消费者对品牌的看法的新方法。


For decades, brand managers have monitored brand health with the use of consumer surveys, which have been refined to address issues related to sampling bias, response bias, leading questions, etc. However, with the advance of Web 2.0 and the internet, consumers have turned to social media to express their opinions on a variety of topics and, subsequently, have generated an extremely large amount of interaction data with brands. Analyzing these publicly available data to measure brand health has attracted great research attention. In this study, we focus on developing a method to measure brand favorability while accounting for the measure biases exhibited by social media posters. Specifically, we propose a probabilistic graphical model-based collective inference framework and implement a block-based Markov chain Monte Carlo sampling technique to obtain an adjusted brand favorability measure that is correlated with traditional survey-based measures used by brands. For analysis, we collect and examine Facebook data for more than 3,300 brands and about 205 million unique users that interact with those brands via their Facebook brand pages. Our data set is large and contains 6.68 billion likes and full text for 1.01 billion user comments, creating challenges for any modeling efforts. We evaluate the effectiveness of our model via out-of-sample prediction, external ground truth testing, and simulation. All demonstrate that our model performs very well, providing brand managers with a new method to more accurately measure consumer opinions toward the brand using social media data.


论文原文：

Kunpeng Zhang, Wendy Moe (2021) Measuring Brand Favorability Using Large-Scale Social Media Data. Information Systems Research 32(4):1128-1139.



4. 竞争与扭曲: 网络借贷市场的信息偏差理论


尽管网络借贷（peer-to-peer, P2P）平台普遍出现，但调查这些平台对P2P市场作用的相关研究仍然滞后。本研究提出了一个模型来研究当存在以下情况时：(1) 贷款回报的不确定性；(2)存在新进入者的竞争时，P2P平台的最佳信息报告策略的市场激励。本研究的重点是在保持平台合理性的同时，由需求方参与者驱动的平台的信息偏差—投资者对风险的乐观/悲观，并描述了平台在不同市场条件下的均衡报告策略。本研究发现，当不确定性很大且进入的威胁很强但并不有害时，平台有动机将信息偏向投资者的偏见信念。这一结果解释了一个可能由竞争和不确定性共同导致信息偏差的现象。然而，一个设计合理的不确定性解决机制可以降低平台的这种动机。本研究通过分析平台决策对P2P网络借贷市场的文献做出了贡献，并为监管P2P网络借贷市场提供了政策意义。


Despite the popular emergence of peer-to-peer (P2P) lending platforms, relevant research investigating the role of these platforms on P2P markets still lags. In this paper, we present a model to study the market incentives of P2P lending platforms’ optimal information-reporting strategies when the following exist: (i) uncertainty on the return of loans and (ii) competition from entrants. We focus on the information bias of platforms driven by demand-side actors—investors’ optimism/pessimism about risk—while we keep the platforms being rational. We characterize platforms’ equilibrium reporting strategies under different market conditions. Surprisingly, we find that when uncertainty is significant, and the threat of entry is strong but not detrimental, the platform has incentives to bias information toward investors’ biased beliefs. This result demonstrates a case where competition and uncertainty may jointly lead to information bias. However, a properly designed uncertainty-resolution mechanism could reduce the incentive. Our findings contribute to the literature on the P2P lending market by analyzing platform decisions and offer policy implications for regulating P2P lending market.


论文原文：

Zhenhua Wu, Lin Hu, Zhijie Lin, Yong Tan (2021) Competition and Distortion: A Theory of Information Bias on the Peer-to-Peer Lending Market. Information Systems Research 32(4):1140-1154.

https://doi.org/10.1287/isre.2020.0956



5. 赢家是谁 ? 平台奖励的理想效果和不良影响研究


本文研究了通过奖励后认可补充者的创新的平台企业的决定。尽管单纯是象征性的，但奖励可能为补充者的产品策略带来激励，最终会带给平台企业理想效果和不良影响。本文从信号理论出发，推导出关于奖励对补充者产品策略影响的假设。为了验证假设，本文在Google Android移动平台和著名的Google Play奖的背景下进行准实验研究，通过估计奖励前后第一名和第二名之间的差异对奖励效果进行判断。研究样本包含2016年至2018年的125个奖项提名者及其793个应用程序。本文发现平台奖励鼓励获奖者专注于发布改进的补充者，而不是发布新的补充者。另外，本文发现平台奖励能够增加了获奖者多头管理的可能性。最后，本文还发现平台奖励通过吸引其他补充者来增加补充市场发布新的补充者。本文通过研究平台奖励的效果，对平台治理文献具有重要贡献，并对理解 “软” 平台治理机制具有理论意义。


We study platform firms’ decision to recognize innovation by complementors ex post through awards. Despite being purely symbolic, awards might set incentives for complementors’ product strategies that can eventually lead to both desirable and undesirable outcomes for the platform firm. We depart from signaling theory and derive hypotheses on the effects of awards on complementors’ product strategies. To test them, we implement a quasi-experiment in the context of the Google Android mobile platform and the prestigious Google Play Award. We infer the effect of the award by estimating the difference-in-differences between award winners and runners-up, before and after the conferral. The main sample encompasses 125 award nominees and their 793 apps between 2016 and 2018. We report three findings. First, the award encourages recipients to focus on releasing complement improvements rather than new complements. Second, the award increases recipients’ likelihood of multihoming. Finally, the award increases new complement releases in the recipients’ market niche by attracting other complementors. We contribute to the platform governance literature by informing about the effects of awards. Additionally, our findings have theoretical implications for understanding “soft” platform governance mechanisms.


论文原文：

Jens Foerderer, Nele Lueker, Armin Heinzl (2021) And the Winner Is …? The Desirable and Undesirable Effects of Platform Awards. Information Systems Research 32(4):1155-1172.

https://doi.org/10.1287/isre.2021.1019



6. 制造商的最优策略：来自电子游戏二手市场的洞察力


与其他类型的信息商品行业相比，电子游戏行业仍然拥有规模可观的二手市场。这一点尤其值得注意，因为一些主要的游戏机公司（比如索尼和微软）实际上拥有完全消灭二手市场的能力；但这些公司似乎已经默许了买卖二手游戏。当然，问题是，在游戏产业中，有什么特殊要素让制造商即使拥有关闭二手市场的技术手短，也会有动力保持一个健康的二手市场？本文利用博弈论模型，调查了在二手游戏市场存在的情况下游戏机对制造商策略的影响。本文发现，当制造商除了提供游戏外，还提供具有价值的电子游戏机时，二手市场增加了制造商的利润，而且不是以消费者为代价的。消费者以及整个社会也会从二手市场中受益。这与没有游戏机或游戏机不提供任何内在价值的情况形成鲜明对比；在这种情况下，制造商会选择关闭二手市场。然而，在有价值的电子游戏机的情况下，二手市场对消费者的日益增加的吸引力可能会同时提高制造商的利润、消费者剩余和社会福利。本文讨论了研究发现以及其对管理和福利事业方面的贡献。


In contrast to industries of other types of information goods, the video game industry still has a sizable secondhand market for games. This is particularly notable because some of the major gaming-console companies (e.g., Sony and Microsoft) actually possess the ability to annihilate the secondhand market altogether; it appears that those companies have given tacit approval to buying and selling used games. Naturally, the question is, what is the special ingredient in the gaming industry that gives a manufacturer incentive to keep a healthy secondhand market even when it has the technological means to shut it down? In this study, leveraging a game-theoretic model, we investigate the effect of gaming console on a manufacturer’s strategy in the presence of a secondhand market for games. We find that when the manufacturer offers a valuable console that provides utilities in addition to playing games, the secondhand market increases the manufacturer’s profit, and that is not at the cost of consumers; the consumers—as well as the society as a whole—also benefit from the secondhand market. This is in stark contrast with settings where there are no consoles involved or the consoles do not offer any intrinsic value; in such settings, the manufacturer would opt to shut down the secondhand market. In the case with a valuable console, however, the increasing appeal of the secondhand market to consumers may improve the manufacturer’s profit, consumer surplus, and social welfare, all at the same time. We discuss our findings along with managerial and welfare implications.


论文原文：

Antino Kim, Rajib L. Saha, Warut Khern-am-nuai (2021) Manufacturer’s “1-Up” from Used Games: Insights from the Secondhand Market for Video Games. Information Systems Research 32(4):1173-1191.



7. 自主工具的不可知性及其边缘使用体验


在关于社会技术系统的现有理论研究中，技术输入和输出之间的关系被假定为人类行为者是可知的，有时是事前的，总是事后的。最近，出现了一批新的自主工具，它们可以独立学习和执行新的动作。然而，这些工具的输入-输出关系对于人类行为者来说，无论是事前还是事后都是不可知的。需要分析人类在与自主工具互动时是如何体验社会物质代理权的设定。为此，本文在世界最大的半导体制造商之一进行了一项探索性的、理论构建的、比较性的案例研究，并调查了芯片设计师是如何与以下两类设计技术互动的：一类是遵循传统的以设计师为中心的方法，设计师知道对工具的特定输入会产生什么样的输出；另一类是则依赖于不断地给用户带来惊喜的自治工具。调查揭示了设计师在使用不同工具时的显著差异。当使用自主工具时，设计师对社会物质代理的体验变得边缘化，这是一种不断涌现的状态，在这种状态下，与工具的互动显得模棱两可，设计沿着多种设计轨迹，按照多样的临时性向前推进。本文扩展了关于社会物质代理的若干主流观点，并对自主工具在未来工作系统中的作用和影响以及在这种情况下如何进行设计和创新进行新的思考。


In the extant theoretical discourse on sociotechnical systems, the relationships between inputs and outputs of technologies are assumed to be knowable to human agents, occasionally ex ante and always ex post. Recently, a new breed of autonomous tools has emerged, which can independently learn and execute novel actions. The input–output relationships of these tools, however, are unknowable to human agents both ex ante and ex post. This calls for analysis of how humans experience the enactment of socio-material agency while interacting with autonomous tools. To this end, we conduct an exploratory, theory-building, comparative case study at one of the world’s largest semiconductor manufacturers. We investigate how chip designers interact with two families of design technologies: one following a traditional designer-centric approach in which the designer knows what outputs particular inputs to the tools will generate and another relying on autonomous tools that continually surprise the user. Our inquiry reveals significant differences in designers’ experiences of using different tools. When using autonomous tools, designers’ experience of enacting socio-material agency becomes liminal, a state of continuous emergence, in which interactions with the tools are marked by ambiguity, and the design is moved forward along multiple design trajectories in accordance with a multifarious temporality. These insights require us to expand upon several dominant views on the enactment of socio-material agency and necessitate novel thinking on the role and impact of autonomous tools in future work systems as well as on how design and innovation proceeds under such conditions.


论文原文：

Zhewei Zhang, Youngjin Yoo, Kalle Lyytinen, Aron Lindberg (2021) The Unknowability of Autonomous Tools and the Liminal Experience of Their Use. Information Systems Research 32(4):1192-1213.



8. 学会创新: 开放式创新中创意生成的互激时空点过程模型


本研究探讨开放式创新平台中创意产生的过程。创意生成过程同时受到多种活动的影响：从参与者之间互动中获取知识，通过持续参与进行刻意实践，以及从失败中学习。由于这些活动之间的动态相互作用，使用简化的回归分析来确定每项活动对创意构思结果的影响具有挑战。为了克服这些挑战，本文采用综合性的实证框架，即具有未观察到的异质性的相互刺激的时空点过程模型，该模型将这些活动在连续时间内的发生内生化，并允许用户依赖效果。通过在开放式创新平台中利用2010年至2016年间13,028名参与者的活动流数据，本文发现这些活动对创意成果的协同效应。研究发现，通过与他人互动获得的知识（例如，刺激性想法）在创意构思过程中起着至关重要的作用，但它们的影响比我们目前所知的更为微妙。与之前认为远距离的类比，即问题领域之外的刺激性想法会刺激创造力的观点不同，本文发现远距离的类比会导致失败。然而，本文进一步发现，这种失败对于创意构思过程是不可或缺的，因为失败会激励创意产生者（1）通过增加他们未来与其他参与者的想法的互动（向他人学习）来获得更多的知识，（2）坚持产生创意，从而提高他们应用所获知识的能力，并确定与他们所获知识储备相关的创新任务（在实践中学习）。本文结果表明，相较于成功，失败在学习活动中具有更强的驱动力。基于研究结果，本文就如何在开放式创新环境中培养创造力提供了见解。


This study investigates the creative idea generation process in an open innovation platform. The idea generation process is simultaneously influenced by multiple activities: knowledge acquisition from participants’ interactions with each other’s ideas, deliberate practice through persistent participation, and learning through failures. Due to the dynamic interplay across these activities, it is challenging to identify each activity’s influence on creative ideation outcomes using reduced-form regression analysis. To overcome these challenges, we employ a comprehensive empirical framework, the mutually exciting spatiotemporal point process model with unobserved heterogeneity, which endogenizes the occurrences of these activities in continuous time and allows for user-dependent effects. By utilizing the activity stream data of 13,028 participants from 2010 to 2016 in an open innovation platform, we uncovered synergistic effects of these activities on creative outcomes. In addition, the relationship between review valence and completion rate can be characterized as a U-shaped pattern, given that reviews arising from negative disconfirmations occur more frequently than those emerging out of positive disconfirmations. The findings also uncover that customers occupying the extreme ends of the completion continuum provide less extreme review ratings. The effect of completion rate on review length is significantly positive. Our text-analysis results suggest that reviews based on sufficient consumption contain more useful insights than do those grounded in incomplete consumption. Moreover, review comments formed after incomplete consumption adversely affect subsequent sales. Finally, we discuss a number of our findings’ implications and provide actionable recommendations that can aid platforms in their efforts to refine their online-review systems and policies in pursuit of enhanced credibility in peer evaluation.


论文原文：

Heeseung Andrew Lee, Angela Aerry Choi, Tianshu Sun, Wonseok Oh (2021) Reviewing Before Reading? An Empirical Investigation of Book-Consumption Patterns and Their Effects on Reviews and Sales. Information Systems Research 32(4):1368-1389.



16.重要的生活地: 本地银行竞争，网上市场借贷，借款人利益的差异


在过去的十年里，在线市场贷款的激增已经颠覆了消费信贷市场，特别是用于债务整合的个人贷款。这些贷款机构（例如Lending Club）超越了当地银行经营的地理界限，为借款人提供同质化的机会和条件。然而，借款人从市场贷款中获得的最终利益可能大有不同，因为当地的替代贷款可能会被用来取代市场贷款，当它们可用且有利的时候。相应地，如果当地银行的竞争促使现有的市场贷款被传统银行贷款所取代，那么市场贷款给所有借款人带来平等利益的承诺就不可能完全实现。这种竞争动态对政策制定也有影响，特别是在判断银行合并和收购的影响方面。本文利用点对点（P2P）借贷平台Lending Club的数据，研究当地市场结构是否促使传统银行将市场借贷的借款人转换为传统银行贷款的异质性激励。研究结果表明，与来自竞争性较弱的市场的借款人相比，居住在竞争性较强的市场的借款人更有可能通过一次性大额支付来提前偿还P2P贷款。因此，来自不同市场的借款人并不能从网络市场借贷扰乱消费信贷市场中获得同等利益。本文对在线市场借贷、其他基于金融科技的市场以及整个消费信贷市场具有重要影响。


In the past decade, the proliferation of online marketplace lending has been disrupting the consumer credit market, especially personal loans used for debt consolidation. These lenders, for example, Lending Club, transcend the geographic boundaries within which local banks operate and offer homogeneous access and terms to borrowers. However, the ultimate benefits borrowers derive from marketplace lending can differ significantly because local alternatives may be used to replace marketplace loans when they are available and favorable. Correspondingly, if local bank competition drives the substitution of an existing marketplace loan with a traditional bank loan, the promise of equal benefits to all borrowers from marketplace lending is unlikely to fully materialize. This competitive dynamic also has implications for policy making, particularly in judging the ramifications of bank mergers and acquisitions. We utilize data from Lending Club, a major peer-to-peer (P2P) lending platform, to study whether local market structure drives heterogeneous incentives of traditional banks to convert borrowers of marketplace lending to conventional bank loans. The results indicate that a borrower who resides in a more competitive market is more likely to pay off a P2P loan early by making a large, one-time payment compared with a borrower from a less competitive market. Thus, borrowers from different markets do not benefit equally from online marketplace lending disrupting the consumer credit market. Our study has implications for online marketplace lending, other FinTech-based markets, and the consumer credit market in general.


论文原文：

Mohammed Alyakoob, Mohammad S. Rahman, Zaiyan Wei (2021) Where You Live Matters: Local Bank Competition, Online Marketplace Lending, and Disparity in Borrower Benefits. Information Systems Research 32(4):1390-1411.



17. 在线零售平台产品列表广告中价格等级对点击率和转化率的影响


针对价格信息在消费者决策中的关键作用，本文调查了价格等级对消费者在购买渠道中对产品列表广告（PLA）反应的影响，以及两个关键词属性的调节作用。采用分层贝叶斯模型，利用电子购物平台中的独特数据集和模拟实验，本文揭示了在购买的早期阶段，消费者更有可能点击价格最值选项（即最高或最低），并将其作为锚点来评估广泛的选项。在购买的后期，当点击倾向于转化为购买时，消费者反而更有可能点击价格适中的选项，这些选项通常是折衷了价格和质量的方案。价格排名的影响在赞助更具体关键词的广告中减弱，在赞助更流行关键词的广告中增加。本文的研究发现为价格信息在PLA背景下的作用提供了新的见解，同时也为制定有效的PLA战略提供了管理意义。


In light of the critical role of price information in consumers’ decision making, this study investigates the effect of price rank on consumers’ responses to product list advertising (PLA) throughout the purchase funnel, as well as the moderating effects of two keyword attributes. A hierarchical Bayesian model, using a unique data set from a leading electronic shopping platform and a simulated experiment, reveals that in the early phases of the purchase funnel, consumers are more likely to click on extremely priced options (i.e., the highest or lowest), which consumers use as anchors to evaluate the broad range of options. Later in the purchase funnel, when clicks tend to convert to purchases, consumers instead are more likely to click on moderately priced options, which usually offer a compromise between price and quality. The effects of price rank diminish among advertisements that sponsor more specific keywords and increase among those that sponsor more popular keywords. These findings provide new insights into the role of price information in the PLA context, as well as managerial implications for devising effective PLA strategies.


论文原文：

Mengzhou Zhuang, Eric (Er) Fang, Jongkuk Lee, Xiaoling Li (2021) The Effects of Price Rank on Clicks and Conversions in Product List Advertising on Online Retail Platforms. Information Systems Research 32(4):1412-1430.



18. 移动消费者扫描技术: 供应链中需求信息学习的组织间信息系统的替代？


供应链上游企业对采用消费者扫描技术（CST）表现出越来越强的兴趣，该技术依赖于消费者通过移动设备扫描购买的产品，作为传统的组织间信息技术（IOIT）的一种新的替代方法来了解下游消费者的需求信息。然而，对于CST如何帮助改善上游企业的绩效和CST是否会取代IOIT，当前文献的研究有限。本文通过建立理论模型来研究CST对供应商的价值，即绕过零售商，采用CST直接收集消费者的扫描信息，并对其扫描行为进行奖励。本文理论分析表明，CST为供应商提供了运营和战略价值。在实践方面，认识到CST收集的扫描信息是已实现需求的审查版本，本文开发了一种简单有效的方法，让供应商从审查的扫描信息中了解已实现的需求。随后，本文检查了该方法的学习效率，最佳的奖励决策，以及由CST带来的供应商库存过剩和不足成本的节省。在战略层面上，本文研究了供应量均衡状态下IOIT和CST的选择。与传统观点相反，研究发现，CST的出现可能会扩大（而不是抑制）供应链内IOIT的使用。研究根据一家实施了CST计划的制造商的真实数据进行模拟分析，本文结果表明CST对制造商的价值是巨大的，并对市场条件如何影响这一价值提供了见解。


Upstream firms in supply chains have shown a growing interest in deploying consumer scanning technology (CST), which relies on consumers scanning the purchased products with their mobile devices, as a novel alternative to conventional interorganizational information technology (IOIT) to learn downstream consumer demand information. However, there is limited understanding in the literature on (i) how CST helps improve upstream firms’ performances and (ii) whether CST will replace IOIT. This paper develops a theoretical model to examine the value of CST to a supplier that bypasses a retailer and employs CST to directly collect scan information from consumers who are incentivized with a reward for their scanning effort. Our theoretical analysis demonstrates that CST offers both operational and strategic value to the supplier. On the operational level, recognizing that the scan information gathered by CST is a censored version of the realized demand, we develop a simple and effective approach for the supplier to learn the realized demand from the censored scan information. We then investigate the learning efficiency of our approach, the optimal reward decisions, and the savings in the supplier’s inventory overage and underage costs arising from CST. On the strategic level, we examine the choice of IOIT and CST within supply chains in equilibrium. Contrary to the conventional view, we find that the availability of CST may expand (instead of suppress) the use of IOIT within supply chains. Using an extensive simulation analysis based on real-world data from a manufacturer that has implemented a CST program, we show that the value of CST to a manufacturer can be substantial and provide insights into how market conditions affect the value.


论文原文：

Ye Shi, Layth C. Alwan, Srinivasan Raghunathan, Yugang Yu, Xiaohang Yue (2021) Mobile Consumer Scanning Technology: A Replacement for Interorganizational Information Systems for Demand Information Learning in Supply Chains?. Information Systems Research 32(4):1431-1449.



19. 共享经济中的商业模式: 在 P2P 租赁市场中制造耐用品


提供使用权而不是转让商品所有权的商业模式已经成为重要的行业趋势，并成为了现有企业面临的挑战。本文分析了点对点（P2P）租赁市场和耐用品制造商之间的互动，强调消费者在使用率方面的异质性在决定制造商倾向于选择哪种商业模式的重要作用。P2P租赁市场的引入产生了一种均衡效应，导致了低使用率消费者的购买。P2P租赁作为一种区别手段，允许制造商对消费者进行细分，提取更大比例的剩余，这可能会伤害消费者。当使用率的异质性处于中等水平时，制造商在P2P租赁中的收益会更好，而当异质性足够高时，消费者在P2P租赁中的收益会更好。本文研究了不同的商业模式，如制造商只有销售，除销售外还有租赁（“双重”公司），在销售的同时还有自己的P2P租赁平台（“P2P赞助”公司），以及制造商通过引入自己的直接租赁与P2P租赁竞争的混合结构（“双重+P2P”公司）。消费者在使用率方面的异质性对商业模式的结果起着根本的作用。当使用率和使用率的异质性足够大时，制造商最好提供销售服务并促进P2P租赁市场的发展。相比之下，如果使用率的异质性太低，制造商更倾向于只提供销售。如果异质性太高，但使用率低于阈值，制造商更愿意作为双重公司经营，直接向消费者提供销售和租赁。如果P2P租赁不可避免，在某些条件下，引入自己的租赁来与P2P租赁竞争可能不是制造商的最佳策略。总的来说，与预期情况相反的是，制造商有动机在多种情况下促进P2P租赁。


Business models that provide access to assets rather than transfer ownership of goods have become an important industry trend, representing a challenge for incumbent firms. This paper analyzes the interaction of a peer-to-peer (P2P) rental market and a manufacturer of durable goods, and highlights the important role of consumer heterogeneity in usage rates in determining which business model would be preferred by the manufacturer. The introduction of a P2P rental market creates an equalizing effect, which leads to purchases from low-usage consumers. P2P rentals act as a discrimination device, allowing the manufacturer to segment consumers and extract a larger fraction of surplus, which might hurt consumers. The manufacturer is better off with P2P rentals when the heterogeneity in usage rates is intermediate, whereas the consumers are better off with P2P rentals when the heterogeneity is sufficiently high. This paper examines different business models such as the manufacturer with only sales, with rentals in addition to sales (the “dual” firm), with its own P2P rentals platform alongside sales (the “P2P-sponsoring” firm), and with a mixed structure in which the manufacturer competes against P2P rentals by introducing its own direct rentals (the “dual-plus-P2P” firm). Consumer heterogeneity in usage rates plays a fundamental role in business model outcomes. When usage rates and heterogeneity in usage rates are sufficiently large, the manufacturer is better off offering sales and facilitating a P2P rental market. In contrast, if heterogeneity in usage rates is too low, the manufacturer prefers to offer only sales. If heterogeneity is too high but usage rates are below a threshold, the manufacturer prefers to operate as a dual firm that offers both sales and rentals directly to consumers. If P2P rentals are unavoidable, introducing its own rentals to compete against P2P rentals might not be the best strategy for the manufacturer under certain conditions. Overall, contrary to what could be expected, the manufacturer has an incentive to facilitate P2P rentals in a large variety of cases.


论文原文：

Vibhanshu Abhishek, Jose A. Guajardo, Zhe Zhang (2021) Business Models in the Sharing Economy: Manufacturing Durable Goods in the Presence of Peer-to-Peer Rental Markets. Information Systems Research 32(4):1450-1469.


20. 用在线产品评级衡量产品类型与购买不确定性: 理论模型及实证应用


基于搜索和体验商品之间的区别，以及纵向和横向水平差异，本文提出了一套以理论为基础、以数据为导向的衡量标准，不仅允许衡量产品类型（搜索与体验，横向与纵向差异化），还可以衡量不确定性的来源以及消费者评论在何种程度上有助于解决不确定性。与已有研究的方法相比，本文所提出的衡量方法具有两个优势：(1)不同于以往将商品分类的方法，本文提出的测量方法是连续的，允许对产品或产品类别中的搜索与体验，横向与纵向的差异化程度进行排序；(2) 本文提出的方法比以往的方法更容易实现，因为它仅依赖于消费者的评级信息（而非专家判断），并且可以在多个层面（属性、产品或产品类别）上使用。使用亚马逊网站的产品评级数据来检验本文所提出的方法的实证应用。本文以数据驱动揭示了在亚马逊的每个产品类别中，适合性在驱动产品效用方面的相对重要性，以及搜索对于确定适合性方面的重要性。研究结果显示，虽然基于经过验证购买者的评级对客观的产品价值是有参考价值的，但目前的亚马逊评论系统似乎对解决适合的不确定性方面的能力有限。本文的研究方法和结果可以促进对产品评论系统的进一步研究以涵盖更多的产品，并能够对产品定位进行定量测量，以支持零售商和制造商的营销策略。


Building on the distinction between search and experience goods, as well as vertical and horizontal differentiation, we propose a set of theory-grounded, data-driven measures that allow us to measure not only product type (search vs. experience, horizontal vs. vertical differentiation) but also sources of uncertainty and to what extent consumer reviews help resolve uncertainty. The proposed measures have two advantages over prior methods: (1) unlike prior categorization schemes that classified goods as either search or experience goods, our measure is continuous, allowing us to rank-order the degree of search versus experience and horizontal versus vertical differentiation among products or categories. (2) Our approach is easier to implement than prior methods, because it relies solely on consumer ratings information (as opposed to expert judgment) and can be employed at multiple levels (attributes, products, or product categories). We illustrate empirical applications of our proposed measures using product rating data from Amazon.com. Our data-driven measures reveal the relative importance of fit in driving product utility and the importance of search for determining fit for each product category at Amazon. Our results also show that, while ratings based on verified purchasers are informative of objective product values, the current Amazon review system appears to have limited ability to resolve fit uncertainty. Our method and findings could facilitate further research on product review systems and enable quantitative measurement of product positioning to support marketing strategy for retailers and manufacturers, covering an expanded group of products.


论文原文：

Peiyu Chen, Lorin M. Hitt, Yili Hong, Shinyi Wu (2021) Measuring Product Type and Purchase Uncertainty with Online Product Ratings: A Theoretical Model and Empirical Application. Information Systems Research 32(4):1470-1489.
