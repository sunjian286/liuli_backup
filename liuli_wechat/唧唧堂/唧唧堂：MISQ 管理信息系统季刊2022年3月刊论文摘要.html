帮助年长员工充分发挥组织潜能:年龄与基于IT的任务绩效的调节中介模型


有证据表明，年长员工在处于基于IT的任务时，表现不如年轻员工。在劳动力迅速老龄化和组织技术激增的时代，这一现象显然令人不安。目前关于这一现象的解释并不清楚，管理者不确定如何才能使年长员工发挥全部潜能。本文提出的研究模型认为，年长员工的信息处理速度下降，是导致他们执行it任务能力下降的原因。模型指出，IT经验和IT自我效能降低了这种下降的负面影响，而IT超载和IT使用的努力成本则加重了这种负面影响。为了检验这个模型，本文使用三个补充研究来收集数据。结果支持了模型，并指出了组织有5种方法，可以用来帮助年长员工提高执行it相关任务的能力。在对人力资源主管的采访中收集的其他数据，证实了这些解决方案的相关性。


Evidence shows that older users have lower performance levels for IT-enabled tasks than younger users. This is alarming at a time when the workforce is rapidly aging and organizational technologies are proliferating. Since the explanation for these lower performance levels remains unclear, managers are not sure how to help older users realize their full potential as contributors to organizational success. The research model presented here identifies the declining information-processing speed of older workers as the cause of their reduced capacity to perform IT-enabled tasks. According to the model, IT experience and IT self-efficacy reduce the negative impacts of this decline, whereas IT overload and the effort cost of IT use aggravate them. To test the model, data were collected using three complementary studies. The results supported the model and indicated five ways that organizations can help older users improve their capacity to perform IT-enabled tasks. Additional data collected in interviews with human resources directors confirmed the relevance of these solutions.


参考文献

Tams S. Helping older workers realize their full organizational potential: a moderated mediation model of age and IT-enabled task performance[J]. MIS Q, 2022.



积极的在线提示是否总能减少用户对赞助研究结果的回避?


用户生成数据的在线社交线索是影响在线用户行为和决策的关键因素之一，具体包括利用用户评论和产品评级等。在线用户分享他们对某个产品(或某个卖家)的评论和评级，成为对同一产品感兴趣的焦点用户的抽象参考组。本研究的重点是赞助搜索结果(SSRs)，一种不请自来的信息，其与用户的搜索查询相匹配，并得到以往消费者的高度评价。以C2C电子商务环境为研究背景，本文研究了积极社交线索在缓解用户搜索产品时，对所遇到的SSR的回避反应方面的作用。本文通过对“回避”文献进行回顾，确定了三种SSR回避形式，即认知回避、行为回避和情感回避。本文利用用户对SSR的隐式关注，来解释用户对所遇到的SSR的回避。此外，本文将社会影响理论扩展到网络环境中，假设抽象参考群体触发社会影响。本文也研究了如何，以及在何种条件下，各种积极的在线社交线索(即与产品和卖家相关的社交线索)可以减少三种形式的SSR回避。通过三个实验，本文证实了用户对SSR的隐式关注和对SSR的回避，并揭示了不同社会线索对减少三种形式SSR回避的不同作用。本文揭示了社会影响在网络环境下减少SSR回避的理论机制，也为在线搜索服务提供商帮助在线用户在搜索过程中做出决策提供了实际的启示。


Online social cues that utilize user-generated data, such as user reviews and product ratings, have become one of the key factors influencing online user behavior and decisions. Online users who shared their reviews and ratings about a product (or a seller) become an   reference group to a focal user interested in the same product. This study focuses on sponsored search results (SSRs), a type of unsolicited information that matches users’ search queries and receives high evaluations from prior consumers. We investigate the effects of positive social cues on alleviating users’ avoidance responses toward an encountered SSR when searching for a product in a C2C e-commerce context. We synthesize the avoidance literature and identify three forms of SSR avoidance, namely, cognitive, behavioral, and affective avoidance. We apply users’ implicit concerns on SSRs to explain users’ avoidance of an encountered SSR. In addition, we extend social influence theory to online settings where   reference groups are posited to trigger social influence. We examine how and under what conditions the three forms of SSR avoidance can be reduced by various positive online social cues (i.e., product- and seller-related). We conduct three laboratory experiments. Results attest to users’ implicit concerns on SSRs and their avoidance of SSRs and reveal different effects of various social cues on reducing the three forms of SSR avoidance. This study uncovers the theoretical mechanisms of social influence on reducing SSR avoidance in online settings. It also offers practical implications for online search service providers to help online users’ decision making in their search process.


参考文献

Honglin D, Weiquan W, Siyuan L I. Can Positive Online Social Cues Always Reduce User Avoidance of Sponsored Search Results?[J]. MIS Quarterly, 2021.



管理集体企业信息系统合规性:社会和绩效管理背景视角


在当今以业务活力和信息技术(IT)进步为特征的环境中，公司必须频繁地更新其企业信息系统(EIS)及其使用策略，以满足不断变化的业务操作。在这种情况下，员工面临着通过不断学习使用EIS的新方法来维护EIS遵从性行为的挑战。此外，由于单元需要通过EIS完成的任务具有相互依赖的性质，因此，对于业务来说，一个功能单元的员工集体维护EIS遵从行为显得尤为必要。然而，员工学习使用EIS难免遇到困难，如何使员工集体达到一定水平，就比较具有挑战性。因此，企业必须建立有效的管理原则，以确保一个职能单位在动态环境中，集体遵守EIS。为了应对这一挑战，本文将社会背景、绩效管理背景的理论视角与社会资本理论相结合，构建了一个解释集体EIS合规的研究模型。模型提出，社会环境通过开发业务It社会资本，增强业务人员和It人员之间的相互学习，积极地影响集体遵守EIS。此外，绩效管理环境对集体遵守EIS有直接和有益的影响，同时对社会环境、业务IT社会资本和集体遵守EIS之间的因果链有间接的调节作用。本文通过对53家使用EIS的159个职能部门的经理和员工，以及他们相应的IT部门经理进行调查，为本研究模型提供了实证支持。本文也讨论了这些发现的理论和实践意义。


In today’s environment characterized by business dynamism and information technology (IT) advances, firms must frequently update their enterprise information systems (EIS) and their use policies to support changing business operations. In this context, users are challenged to maintain EIS compliance behavior by continuously learning new ways of using EIS. Furthermore, it is imperative to businesses that employees of a functional unit maintain EIS compliance behavior collectively, due to the interdependent nature of tasks that the unit needs to accomplish through EIS. However, it is particularly challenging to achieve such a collective level of EIS compliance, due to the difficulty that these employees may encounter in quickly learning updated EIS. It is, therefore, vital for firms to establish effective managerial principles to ensure collective EIS compliance of a functional unit in a dynamic environment. To address this challenge, this study develops a research model to explain collective EIS compliance by integrating theoretical lens on social context and performance management context with social capital theory. It proposes that social context, an organizational environment characterized by trust and support, positively affects collective EIS compliance by developing business–IT social capital that enhances mutual learning between business and IT personnel. Furthermore, the performance management context, an organizational environment characterized by discipline and “stretch,” is seen to have a direct and beneficial effect on collective EIS compliance as well as an indirect, moderating effect on the causal chain among social contexts, business–IT social capital, and collective EIS compliance. General empirical support for this research model is provided via a multiple-sourced survey of managers and employees of 159 functional units of 53 firms that use EIS, as well as their corresponding IT unit managers. The theoretical and practical implications of these findings are discussed.


参考文献

Zhou J, Fang Y, Grover V. Managing Collective Enterprise Information Systems Compliance: A Social and Performance Management Context Perspective[J]. MIS Quarterly, 2022.



移动应用中推荐策略的异质需求效应:来自计量经济学模型和机器学习工具的证据


本文研究了移动渠道中各种推荐策略的有效性，以及其对消费者关于单个产品的效用和需求水平的影响。研究发现，不同的推荐策略在有效性上存在显著差异，直接嵌入被推荐替代方案的社会证明的推荐策略，其表现优于其他推荐。此外，由于规定的时间多样性，将社会证明与更高水平的诱导意识相结合的推荐策略，对移动渠道的影响更强。本文还研究了不同物品、不同用户和不同情境下的需求效应的异质性，进一步验证了上述信息和说服机制，得到了丰富的见解。本文利用机器学习方法，在存在内生性的情况下，促进因果效应的估计具体而言，本文开发了新的计量经济工具——基于用户生成评论的深度学习模型来捕获产品差异化。实证研究结果扩展了关于推荐系统异质影响的现有知识，调和了相关文献中相互矛盾的先前结果，具有重要的商业含义。


In this paper, we examine the effectiveness of various recommendation strategies in the mobile channel and their impact on consumers’ utility and demand levels for individual products. We find significant differences in effectiveness among various recommendation strategies. Interestingly, recommendation strategies that directly embed social proofs for the recommended alternatives outperform other recommendations. In addition, recommendation strategies combining social proofs with higher levels of induced awareness due to the prescribed temporal diversity have an even stronger effect on the mobile channel. We also examine the heterogeneity of the demand effect across items, users, and contextual settings, further verifying empirically the aforementioned information and persuasion mechanisms and generating rich insights. We facilitate the estimation of causal effects in the presence of endogeneity using machine-learning methods. Specifically, we develop novel econometric instruments that capture product differentiation (isolation) based on deep-learning models of user-generated reviews. Our empirical findings extend the current knowledge regarding the heterogeneous impact of recommender systems, reconcile contradictory prior results in the related literature, and have significant business implications.


参考文献

Adamopoulos P, Ghose A, Tuzhilin A. Heterogeneous demand effects of recommendation strategies in a mobile application: Evidence from econometric models and machine-learning instruments[J]. MIS Quarterly (Forthcoming), 2016.



利用智能移动健康平台增强患者的能力:随机现场实验的证据


随着科技的进步，移动电话和可穿戴设备已经成为“日益普及的智能数字基础设施”的延伸。本文研究了移动健康(mHealth)平台及其对慢性病患者预后的健康和经济影响。为此，作者与一家大型移动健康公司合作，该公司是亚洲最大的移动健康应用程序平台之一，专门从事糖尿病护理。本文设计并实施了一项随机现场实验，该实验基于1,070名糖尿病患者详细的健康活动(例如，步行、锻炼、睡眠、食物摄入)和几个月来的血糖值。本文主要研究结果表明，采用mHealth应用程序可以改善健康行为，从而改善短期指标(如患者血糖和糖化血红蛋白水平的降低)和长期指标(如住院次数和医疗费用)。使用mHealth应用程序的患者每天锻炼更久，吃更健康、更低卡路里的食物，步数更多，睡眠更久，也更有可能用远程医疗来代替线下访问。将同一款应用的移动版本和PC版本进行比较，结果表明，移动版本比PC版本更能帮助患者在饮食、锻炼和生活方式等方面做出行为调整，从而最终改善他们的健康状况。本文还将平台提供个性化健康提醒与通用(非个性化)提醒的效果进行对比，结果令人惊讶，个性化健康提醒对患者应用程序的参与度和生活方式的改变产生较小的影响，导致健康改善程度较低。但患者更倾向于用远程医疗来替代线下访问。在实现更好的医疗保健结果方面，移动医疗技术和平台设计具有巨大的潜力。 


With today’s technological advancements, mobile phones and wearable devices have become extensions of an increasingly diffused and smart digital infrastructure. In this paper, we examine mobile health (mHealth) platforms and their health and economic impacts on the outcomes of chronic disease patients. To do so, we partnered with a major mHealth firm that provides one of the largest mobile health app platforms in Asia specializing in diabetes care. We designed and implemented a randomized field experiment based on detailed patient health activities (e.g., steps, exercises, sleep, food intake) and blood glucose values from 1,070 diabetes patients over several months. Our main findings show that the adoption of the mHealth app leads to an improvement in health behavior, which in turn leads to both short term metrics (such as reduction in patients’ blood glucose and glycated hemoglobin levels) and longer-term metrics (such as hospital visits and medical expenses). Patients who adopted the mHealth app undertook higher levels of exercise, consumed healthier food with lower calories, walked more steps and slept for longer times on a daily basis. They also were more likely to substitute offline visits with telehealth. A comparison of mobile versus PC-enabled versions of the same app demonstrates that the mobile version has a stronger effect than PC version in helping patients make these behavioral modifications with respect to diet, exercise, and lifestyle, which ultimately leads to an improvement in their healthcare outcomes. We also compared outcomes when the platform facilitates personalized health reminders to patients vis-à-vis generic (non-personalized) reminders. Surprisingly, we found that personalized mobile messages with patient-specific guidance can have an inadvertent (smaller) effect on patient app engagement and lifestyle changes, leading to a lower health improvement. However, they are more like to encourage a substitution of offline visits by telehealth. Overall, our findings indicate the massive potential of mHealth technologies and platform design in achieving better healthcare outcomes.


参考文献

Ghose A, Guo X, Li B, et al. Empowering patients using smart mobile health platforms: Evidence from a randomized field experiment[J]. Forthcoming at MIS Quarterly, 2021.



实时拼车市场里劳动力供给的峰时定价和短期工资弹性


优步和Lyft等实时拼车服务的兴起，改变了传统行业的格局。本研究利用中国主要拼车平台的数据，对实时拼车市场劳动力供给的短期工资弹性进行了全面分析。通过使用不均衡驾驶限制的外生冲击作为工具变量，本文发现拼车司机的劳动供给弹性为负，这表明司机在平均小时工资较高的日子往往驾驶更少。具体而言，每小时工资增加一个百分点，每天的工作时间将减少0.931%。这一发现与基于参照依赖偏好理论的行为收入目标模型相一致:司机自己设定了每天要赚的小目标，当他们低于收入目标时比高于收入目标时更有动力提供劳动力。因此，在每小时收入高的日子，司机会少工作，一旦达到收入目标，他们就会退出市场。此外，本文发现出拼车司机更理性，具有正的劳动供给弹性，这意味着司机在有重复的学习机会时更理性。估算劳动力供给弹性，对于拼车平台管理者理解各种高峰定价算法和司机补贴计划的经济效率至关重要。本研究表明，对所有拼车司机采取统一涨价或司机补贴的方式可能不能有效地激励司机的劳动力供应。


The prominence of real-time ridesharing services, such as Uber and Lyft, has dramatically changed the landscape of traditional industries. This study provides a comprehensive analysis of the short-term wage elasticity of labor supply in real-time ridesharing markets using data from a major ridesharing platform in China. By exploiting an exogenous shock from uneven driving restrictions as an instrumental variable, we find a negative labor supply elasticity for ridesharing drivers, suggesting that drivers tend to drive less during days with a higher average hourly wage. Specifically, a percent increase in hourly wage will lead to a 0.931 percent decrease in daily working hours. This surprising finding is consistent with the behavioral income-targeting model based on the theory of reference-dependent preferences: Drivers have heuristic daily targets for total earnings and are more motivated to supply labor when they are below their income target than when they are above it. Therefore, they work less on days when earnings per hour are high and quit the market once their income target is reached. In addition, we find that taxi drivers are more rational and have positive labor supply elasticity, which implies that drivers are more rational when they have repeated opportunities for learning. Estimating labor supply elasticity is critical to understanding the economic efficiency of various surge pricing algorithms and driver subsidization programs for ridesharing platforms and policymakers. Our research suggests that a uniform price surging or driver subsidization approach for all ridesharing drivers may not incentivize the labor supply of drivers effectively.


参考文献

He S, Qiu L, Cheng X. Surge pricing and short-term wage elasticity of labor supply in real-time ridesharing markets[J]. Forthcoming in MIS Quarterly, 2021.



社会警觉性和社会传播的算法过程:机器人如何在Twitter上传播信息


尽管对机器人的关注有所增加，人们对机器人以及它们如何在社交媒体上传播信息仍知之甚少。本文利用管道中介的视角和多个案例研究的结果，开发了一个新的算法管道中介框架，以理解机器人的信息传播和可能影响其行为的设计选择。算法管道经纪包括两个相互交织的过程。第一个过程，算法社会警觉性，依赖于机器人活动来管理和重新配置信息。算法社交警觉性非常重要，因为它涉及行动触发器，指示机器人正在搜索、发现和检索的信息类型。第二个过程，算法社会传播，依靠机器人活动来美化和分发策划好的信息。算法社交传播很重要，因为它可以通过增加可发现性和定向目标，扩大机器人传播信息的范围。本文提供的两种算法管道经纪流程是机器人特有的，不同于最初的基于人类活动的管道经纪概念。首先，由于机器人缺乏人类的意义构建能力，由自动化和行动触发器驱动，而不是由情感驱动，因此算法管道中介比人类管道中介更稳定、更可靠。第二，自动化提高了信息管理和传递的速度和规模，使得算法管道经纪不仅更加一致，而且更加快速和广泛。第三，算法管道经纪包括一组新的概念(例如，行动触发器和快速扩展)，这些概念是特定于机器人的，因此不适用于人类管道经纪。


Despite increased empirical attention, theory on bots and how they act to disseminate information on social media remains poorly understood. Our study leverages the conduit brokerage perspective and the findings of a multiple case study to develop a novel framework of algorithmic conduit brokerage for understanding information dissemination by bots and the design choices that may influence their actions. Algorithmic conduit brokerage encompasses two intertwined processes. The first process, algorithmic social alertness, relies on bot activity to curate and reconfigure information. Algorithmic social alertness is significant because it involves action triggers that dictate the kinds of information being searched, discovered, and retrieved by bots. The second process, algorithmic social transmission, relies on bot activity to embellish and distribute the information curated. Algorithmic social transmission is important because it can broaden the reach of information disseminated by bots through increased discoverability and directed targeting. The two algorithmic conduit brokerage processes we offer are unique to bots and distinct from the original conceptualization of conduit brokerage, which is rooted in human activity. First, since bots lack the human ability of sensemaking and are instead fueled by automation and action triggers rather than by emotions, algorithmic conduit brokerage is more invariant and reliable than human conduit brokerage. Second, automation increases the speed and scale of information curation and transfer, making algorithmic conduit brokerage not only more consistent but also faster and more extensive. Third, algorithmic conduit brokerage includes a set of new concepts (e.g., action triggers and rapid scaling) that are specific to bots and therefore not applicable to human conduit brokerage.


参考文献

Salge C A L, Karahanna E, Thatcher J B. Algorithmic processes of social alertness and social transmission: how bots disseminate information on twitter[J]. MIS Quarterly, 2022, 46(1).



互惠还是利益?利用数字社会关系促进健康行为


本文研究了数字社交平台对线下健康行为的互惠作用。虽然互惠是人类心理学的一个基本方面，但它在促进健康行为方面的应用还很有限。本文在移动社交网络平台上，对1700多对用户进行了现场随机实验。在互惠治疗组中，参与者会收到朋友赠送的礼物，并被要求通过参加跑步挑战来回报朋友的好意，将他们的表现与自利激励下的表进行比较。基于社会交换理论，本文认为，基于互惠的激励在激励行为改变方面，优于具有适度奖励的自利激励。平均看来，互惠性在诱导运动行为方面比自利性强很多。此外，研究结果表明，互惠效应的大小取决于发送者和接收者之间的社会亲密程度。社会亲密度对互惠效应的影响呈倒u型：当亲密程度适度时，这种效果最强;当亲密程度过于强烈或过弱时，这种效果就会减弱。与基于自身利益的经济激励相比，本文的发现为促进健康行为的机制设计提供了一个潜在的更强大的途径。


We examine the role of reciprocity enabled by digital social platforms for offline healthy behavior. Although reciprocity is a fundamental aspect of human psychology, its application in promoting healthy behavior has been limited. We conduct a randomized field experiment with over 1,700 pairs of users on a mobile social network platform. Individuals in the reciprocity treatment group receive a gift from their friends and are asked to return this favor by participating in a running challenge. Their performance is compared to the self-interest incentives widely used in practice. Building on social exchange theory, we argue that reciprocity-based incentives will outperform self-interest incentives with modest reward for motivating behavior change. We find that, on average, reciprocity is stronger than self-interest in inducing exercise behavior by a substantial amount. Furthermore, our results reveal that the magnitude of the reciprocity effect is contingent on the social closeness between senders and receivers. Interestingly, social closeness has an inverted U-shaped influence on the reciprocity effect. The effect is strongest when closeness is moderate, and wanes when closeness is either too strong or too weak. Compared to commonly used self-interest based financial incentives, our findings offer a potentially more powerful avenue for mechanism design in promoting healthy behavior.


参考文献

Interestingly, receiving rewards does not significantly affect the valence of subsequent reviews. Our study extends past research by being one of the first to examine how receiving performance-contingent rewards affects subsequent behaviors of reviewers. Our results can also guide platform managers to design efficient and effective incentive policies.


参考文献

Yu Y, Khern-am-nuai W, Pinsonneault A. When paying for reviews pays off: The case of performance-contingent monetary rewards[J]. MIS Quarterly (Forthcoming), 2020.



个性化推荐与综合评分对消费后偏好反应的影响


在线零售商利用产品评级来表明产品的质量，并帮助消费者确定要购买的产品。这些评级通常采取非个性化、综合产品评级的形式(许多消费者对产品的平均评级，例如基于100条评论的平均评级为4.5/5)，或对产品的个性化预测偏好评级(推荐系统生成的关于消费者对产品评级的预测，比如我们认为你会给这个产品打分4.5/5)。两种形式的评级都可以为消费者提供决策帮助，但两种形式传达的产品质量信息类型不同，运行的心理机制也不同。之前的研究表明，每种推荐类型都能显著影响消费者的体验后偏好评级，构成判断偏见，但没有比较这两种常见的产品评级格式的影响。通过实验，本文发现，当单独显示时，汇总评级和个性化推荐会在体验后偏好评级上产生类似的偏差。一起显示时，效果没有累积增加。相反，个性化推荐往往占主导地位。本文的研究结果可以帮助零售商确定如何使用这些不同类型的产品评级，以最有效地服务于他们的客户。此外，这些结果有助于教育消费者产品评级显示如何影响他们的陈述偏好。


Online retailers use product ratings to signal quality and help consumers identify products for purchase. These ratings commonly take the form of either non-personalized, aggregate product ratings (i.e., the average rating a product received from a number of consumers such as “the average rating is 4.5/5 based on 100 reviews”), or personalized predicted preference ratings for a product (i.e., recommender-system-generated predictions for a consumer’s rating of a product such as “we think you’d rate this product 4.5/5”). Ratings in either format can provide decision aid to the consumer, but the two formats convey different types of product quality information and operate with different psychological mechanisms. Prior research has indicated that each recommendation type can significantly affect consumer’s post-experience preference ratings, constituting a judgmental bias, but has not compared the effects of these two common product-rating formats. Using a laboratory experiment, we show that aggregate ratings and personalized recommendations create similar biases on post-experience preference ratings when shown separately. Shown together, there is no cumulative increase in the effect. Instead, personalized recommendations tend to dominate. Our findings can help retailers determine how to use these different types of product ratings to most effectively serve their customers. Additionally, these results help to educate the consumer on how product-rating displays influence their stated preferences.


参考文献

Adomavicius G, Bockstedt J, Curley S, et al. Effects of Personalized Recommendations versus Aggregate Ratings on Post-Consumption Preference Responses[J]. Forthcoming, MIS Quarterly, 2021.



在商业智能环境中应用和扩展有效使用理论


组织对信息系统的使用效率会影响其从中获取的好处。尽管知道如何有效地使用信息系统很重要，但关于该主题的理论却很少。有效使用理论（TEU）是一个未经测试的理论。本文在一个相当重要的情景——商业智能(BI)情景，对TEU进行情境化、扩展和测试。本文使用了混合方法和三阶段方法，包括仪器开发(n = 218)、两次横断面调查(n = 437)和三组后续访谈(n = 33)。本文的贡献有三点：（1）展示了如何将TEU进行情境化、操作化和扩展；（2）证明TEU的许多预测在BI环境中成立，同时还揭示了改进理论的方法；（3）为高管提供可以在组织中改进BI的使用的方法。


The benefits that organizations accrue from information systems depend on how effectively the systems are used. Yet despite the importance of knowing what it takes to use information systems effectively, little theory on the topic exists. One recent and largely untested exception is the theory of effective use (TEU). We report on a contextualization, extension, and test of TEU in the business intelligence (BI) context, a context of considerable importance in which researchers have called for such studies. We used a mixed-method, three-phase approach involving instrument development (n = 218), two-wave cross-sectional survey (n = 437), and three sets of follow-up interviews (n = 33). The paper contributes by (1) showing how TEU can be contextualized, operationalized, and extended, (2) demonstrating that many of TEU’s predictions hold in the BI context while also revealing ways to improve the theory, and (3) offering practical insights executives can draw on to improve use of BI in their organizations.


参考文献

Trieu V H, Burton-Jones A, Green P, et al. Applying and extending the theory of effective use in a business intelligence context[J]. MIS Quarterly, 2022, 46(1): 645-678.




组织如何从信息系统事件中学习？过去、现在和未来的综合


本文对有关组织如何从信息系统(IS)事件中学习的相关文献进行回顾。本文确定了三种学习模式，这取决于构成学习过程的实践、在学习中扮演角色的特定参与者、学习实践的时间导向和学习的特定语境焦点。文献主要侧重于从过去的经验中学习，为未来的事件吸取教训(反思学习模式)。然而，越来越多的文献强调通过参与当前事件来学习(嵌入式学习模式)，少数研究表明，组织可以前瞻性地学习，为未来事件做准备(前瞻性学习模式)。本文认为，尽管这三种学习模式是有效的，但并不能充分解释组织在孤立使用时，是如何从IS事件中进行学习。由于IS事件越来越多地作为跨信息系统和组织环境的交互事件集展开，因此需要将组织学习理论化为这些学习模式之间的迭代过程。本文将这三种学习模式综合成一个综合框架，并对它们的支持和抑制关系进行理论化。本文提出了一些未来研究的机会，这将促进学界对“组织如何从IS事件中学习”的理解。


We review the literature on how organizations learn from information system (IS) incidents. We identify three modes of learning depending on the practices that constitute the learning process, the specific actors who play roles in learning, the temporal orientation of the learning practices, and the specific contextual focus of the learning. The literature focuses primarily on learning from past experience to draw lessons for future incidents (reflective learning mode). Yet, a growing stream of literature stresses the importance of learning through engagement with present incidents (embedded learning mode), and a few studies suggest that organizations can learn prospectively to prepare for future incidents (prospective learning mode). We argue that although these three learning modes are effective, they do not adequately explain how organizations learn from IS incidents when used in isolation. Since IS incidents unfold increasingly as sets of interacting events across information systems and organizational settings, organizational learning needs to be theorized as an iterative process among these learning modes. We synthesize these three learning modes into an integrative framework and theorize about their supportive and inhibiting relations. We suggest some opportunities for future research, which would advance our understanding of how organizations learn from IS incidents.


参考文献

Mehrizi M H R, Nicolini D, Mòdol J R. How do organizations learn from information system incidents? a synthesis of the past, present, and future[J]. MIS Quarterly, 2022, 46(1).
