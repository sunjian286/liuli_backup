1、抵押品激增与信息枯竭

Collateral Booms and Information Depletion


我们提出了一个适用于信贷繁荣时期的信息生产的新理论。企业家需要通过借贷投资项目，其中一些项目可以使其转移资源。贷款人可以通过两种方式防止资源转移: 抵押或者采用产生持久项目信息的高成本筛选。在均衡中，抵押-筛选组合取决于总抵押的价值。较高的抵押价值能够使资源重新分配到生产性项目，但也由此挤出了筛选。这具有重要的动态含义：在高抵押物价值驱动的信贷繁荣时期(如房地产繁荣)，经济活动扩张，但经济中现有项目的信息储备会枯竭。因此，抵押品驱动的繁荣以深度危机和缓慢复苏告终: 当繁荣结束时，投资受到抵押品和现有项目信息的限制，而这需要时间重建。我们利用美国公司层面的数据为这一机制提供了实证支持。


We develop a new theory of information production during credit booms. Entrepreneurs need credit to undertake investment projects, some of which enable them to divert resources. Lenders can protect themselves from such diversion in two ways: collateralization, and costly screening that generates durable information about projects. In equilibrium, the collateralization-screening mix depends on the value of aggregate collateral. High collateral values make it possible to reallocate resources towards productive projects, but they also crowd out screening. This has important dynamic implications. During credit booms driven by high collateral values (e.g. real estate booms), economic activity expands but the economy’s stock of information on existing projects gets depleted. As a result, collateral-driven booms end in deep crises and slow recoveries: when booms end, investment is constrained both by the lack of collateral and by the lack of information on existing projects, which takes time to rebuild. We provide empirical support for the mechanism using US firm-level data.


参考文献：

Asriyan, V., Laeven, L., & Martin, A. (2022). Collateral booms and information depletion. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 517-555.



2、出租车行业的空间均衡、搜索摩擦和动态效率

Spatial Equilibrium, Search Frictions, and Dynamic Efficiency in the Taxi Industry


本文分析了出租车的动态空间平衡，并展示了常见的出租车法规如何通过搜索摩擦和错误分配导致低效率。为分析监管对摩擦和效率的作用，我提出了出租车和乘客之间空间搜索和空间匹配的动态模型。使用纽约市黄色徽章出租车的综合数据集，我采用该动态模型计算了空置出租车的均衡空间分布，并在给定价格和徽章法规的情况下估计日内需求。估计显示，纽约市场平日实现收益总额约为570万美元（平均单次行程约 27 美元），但还有53000名顾客因搜索摩擦而找不到出租车。反事实分析表明，实施简单的关税定价改革可以提高分配效率并扩大市场，提供每日高达22.7万美元的消费者剩余和每日高达4.9万次额外出租车-乘客配对，这与采用完美静态匹配模式生成的匹配收益相当。


This article analyses the dynamic spatial equilibrium of taxicabs and shows how common taxi regulations lead to substantial inefficiencies as a result of search frictions and mis-allocation. To analyse the role of regulation on frictions and efficiency, I pose a dynamic model of spatial search and matching between taxis and passengers. Using a comprehensive dataset of New York City yellow medallion taxis, I use this model to compute the equilibrium spatial distribution of vacant taxis and estimate intraday demand given price and medallion regulations. My estimates show that the weekday New York market achieves about $\$$ 5.7 million in daily welfare or about  $\$$ 27 per trip, but an additional 53 thousand customers fail to find cabs due to search frictions. Counterfactual analysis shows that implementing simple tariff pricing changes can enhance allocative efficiency and expand the market, offering daily consumer surplus gains of up to  $\$$ 227 thousand and up to 49 thousand additional daily taxi-passenger matches, a similar magnitude to the gain in matches generated by adopting a perfect static matching technology.


参考文献：

Buchholz, N. (2022). Spatial equilibrium, search frictions, and dynamic efficiency in the taxi industry. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 556-591.



3、技能偏向的结构变化

Skill-Biased Structural Change


通过对一系列发达经济体的研究，我们发现人均GDP的增长与高技能劳动力密集型部门的附加值构成的系统性转变有关，我们将这一过程称为技能偏向的结构性变化。由此，这些经济体的进一步发展导致了对熟练劳动力相对需求的增加。我们构建了这一过程的两部门定量模型，并以此评估1977年至2005年期间美国和其他十个发达经济体的技能溢价上升的原因。对于美国，我们发现技术变革中特定行业的技能中性成分解释了技术变革导致的技能溢价总增长的 18-24%，并且该技术中性成分通过技能偏向的结构变化影响技能溢价。


Using a broad panel of advanced economies, we document that increases in GDP per capita are associated with a systematic shift in the composition of value added to sectors that are intensive in high-skill labour, a process we label as skill-biased structural change. It follows that further development in these economies leads to an increase in the relative demand for skilled labour. We develop a quantitative two-sector model of this process as a laboratory to assess the sources of the rise of the skill premium in the U.S. and a set of ten other advanced economies, over the period 1977 to 2005. For the U.S., we find that the sector-specific skill neutral component of technical change accounts for 18–24% of the overall increase of the skill premium due to technical change, and that the mechanism through which this component of technical change affects the skill premium is via skill-biased structural change.


参考文献：

Buera, F. J., Kaboski, J. P., Rogerson, R., & Vizcaino, J. I. (2022). Skill-biased structural change. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 592-625.



4、风险匹配

Risky Matching


我们构建了一个模型，在该模型中，规避风险的工人可以在进入不同公司之前对自己进行高成本的技能投资。在投资阶段，工人面临多种风险来源。他们不确定自己的技能水平，也不确定他们在就业时的收入冲击。本文分析了该模型两个版本的均衡结果，模型的均衡结果取决于不确定性何时解决，这两个均衡也决定了工人和公司之间的风险分担比例。我们对关于风险、工人和公司以及技术四者的比较静态均衡进行了全面分析。我们推导出匹配输出函数和风险态度在哪些条件下的转变会导致更多投资，并分析了它们将如何影响匹配和工资。为检验该理论的应用相关性，该研究对模型进行了程式化的定量评估，并分析了美国工资不平等加剧的来源（风险、异质性或技术）。本文发现，风险的变化是不平等加剧的最重要驱动因素，其次是技术变革。我们表明，如果忽略本文模型的关键特征，即教育投资是内生的，这些结论就会发生显著变化。


We develop a model where risk-averse workers can costly invest in their skills before matching with heterogenous firms. At the investment stage, workers face multiple sources of risk. They are uncertain about how skilled they will turn out and also about their income shock realizations at the time of employment. We analyse the equilibria of two versions of the model that depend on when uncertainty resolves, which determines the available risk-sharing possibilities between workers and firms. We provide a thorough analysis of equilibrium comparative statics regarding changes in risk, worker and firm heterogeneity, and technology. We derive conditions on the match output function and risk attitudes under which these shifts lead to more investment and show how this affects matching and wages. To illustrate the applied relevance of our theory, we provide a stylized quantitative assessment of the model and analyse the sources (risk, heterogeneity, or technology) of rising U.S. wage inequality. We find that changes in risk were the most important driver behind the surge in inequality, followed by technological change. We show that these conclusions are significantly altered if one neglects the key feature of our model, which is that educational investment is endogenous.


参考文献：

Chade, H., & Lindenlaub, I. (2022). Risky matching. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 626-665.



5、内生增长模型中的结构中断

Structural Breaks in an Endogenous Growth Model


我们研究了结构中断带来的不确定性范围的影响。在我们的模型中，代理人对冲击过程中的政权转变所带来的不确定性的反应，与他们对感受到的冲击波动性的反应不同。具体而言，他们对与结构中断相关的全要素生产率的不确定性的反应要大于对感受到的波动性的反应。这是因为当冲击改变信念和感知财富时，终生效用变化更大。


We study the effects of parameter uncertainty prompted by structural breaks. In our model, agents respond differently to uncertainty prompted by regime shifts in shock processes than they react to comparable perceived increases in shock volatility. The magnitude of the response to an increase in uncertainty about TFP associated with a structural break is greater than that of a response to a comparable perceived rise in volatility. This is because lifetime utility varies more when shocks shift beliefs and perceived wealth.


参考文献：

Cogley, T., & Jovanovic, B. (2022). Structural Breaks in an Endogenous Growth Model. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 666-694.




6、腐败和企业

 Corruption and Firms 


我们利用具有丰富的腐败和企业信息的微观数据，估计了反腐打击运动对巴西地方政府的因果影响。在反腐败审计之后，市政当局在最依赖政府关系和公共采购部门的公司数量有所增加。通过对地理溢出效应的估计和检验表明，审计通过直接探测效应和间接威慑渠道发挥作用。有政治关系的公司在审计之后会受到影响。我们的估计表明，反腐计划产生了显著的地方乘数，这与对依赖政府的公司征收巨额腐败税的原理是一致的。


We estimate the causal real economic effects of a randomized anti-corruption crackdown on local governments in Brazil using rich micro-data on corruption and firms. After anti-corruption audits, municipalities experience an increase in the number of firms concentrated in sectors most dependent on government relationships and public procurement. Through the estimation of geographic spillovers and additional tests, we show that audits operate via both a direct detection effect as well as through indirect deterrence channels. Politically connected firms suffer after the audits. Our estimates indicate the anti-corruption program generates significant local multipliers which are consistent with the presence of a large corruption tax on government-dependent firms.


参考文献：

Colonnelli, E., & Prem, M. (2022). Corruption and firms. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 695-732.



7、国际溢出效应和本地信贷周期

International Spillovers and Local Credit Cycles 


本文研究了2003-2013年全球金融周期(GFC)对土耳其这一大型新兴市场的国内信贷市场的影响。我们将涵盖了所有企业信贷交易的管理数据与银行资产负债表匹配，并证明了四个事实:(1)全球金融环境的缓解导致借贷成本降低和本地贷款增加;(2)国际资本市场敞口较大的国内银行将全球金融危机转移到了本地;(3)由于未覆盖利率平价溢价(UIP)与GFC的共同作用，本币借贷成本的下降大于外币借贷成本的下降;(4)新发放贷款的抵押数据显示，在全球金融危机的繁荣期，抵押约束并未放松。


This article studies the transmission of the Global Financial Cycle (GFC) to domestic credit market conditions in a large emerging market, Turkey, over 2003–13. We use administrative data covering the universe of corporate credit transactions matched to bank balance sheets to document four facts: (1) an easing in global financial conditions leads to lower borrowing costs and an increase in local lending; (2) domestic banks more exposed to international capital markets transmit the GFC locally; (3) the fall in local currency borrowing costs is larger than foreign currency borrowing costs due to the co-movement of the uncovered interest rate parity (UIP) premium with the GFC over time; (4) data on posted collateral for new loan issuances show that collateral constraints do not relax during the boom phase of the GFC.


参考文献：

Di Giovanni, J., Kalemli-Özcan, Ş., Ulu, M. F., & Baskaya, Y. S. (2022). International spillovers and local credit cycles. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 733-773.



8、罗马交通网络连接与经济一体化

 Roman Transport Network Connectivity and Economic Integration


我们发现罗马时代第一个综合多式联运泛欧运输网的建立影响了2000多年的经济一体化。利用出土的罗马陶瓷在空间上高度分散的数据，我们证明了当代的区域间贸易受到了网络连通性的影响。直至今日，这些连通性通过近似于跨区域的企业投资行为继续影响着贸易整合。由于偏好和价值观的双边趋同，较好的基础设施路线和文化融合可以为连通性提供一部分解释。本文证实了该现象出于罗马特定的连通性，而不是使用前罗马贸易数据已有交换模式的结果。


We show that the creation of the first integrated multi-modal pan-European transport network during Roman times influences economic integration over two millennia. Drawing on spatially highly disaggregated data on excavated Roman ceramics, we document that contemporary interregional trade was influenced by connectivity within the network. Today, these connectivity differentials continue to influence integration as approximated by cross-regional firm investment behaviour. Continuity is partly explained by selective infrastructure routing and cultural integration due to bilateral convergence in preferences and values. We show that our results are Roman-connectivity specific and do not reflect pre-existing patterns of exchange using pre-Roman trade data.


参考文献：

Flückiger, M., Hornung, E., Larch, M., Ludwig, M., & Mees, A. (2022). Roman transport network connectivity and economic integration. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 774-810.



9、从移民到美国人：大迁徙期间的种族和同化

From Immigrants to Americans: Race and Assimilation during the Great Migration


一个新的少数群体的到来如何影响现有少数群体的社会接受度和其他结果？我们在第一次大迁徙的背景下研究该问题。1915年至1930年间，150万非洲裔美国人从美国南部迁移到北部城市中心，这些城市中心是过去几十年来数百万欧洲移民的家园。我们实证检验了这样一个假设，即美国黑人的流入改变了本土出生的白人对外群体距离的看法，并减少了欧洲移民社会融合的障碍。使用shift-share工具变量预测黑人移民，我们发现居住在接受更多黑人移民地区的移民经历了一系列更高程度的同化，例如入籍率提高和与本地出生的群体通婚。来自历史媒体的证据和移民国籍的异质性特征为以上结果提供了额外支持。


How does the arrival of a new minority group affect the social acceptance and outcomes of existing minorities? We study this question in the context of the First Great Migration. Between 1915 and 1930, 1.5 million African Americans moved from the U.S. South to Northern urban centres, which were home to millions of European immigrants arrived in previous decades. We formalize and empirically test the hypothesis that the inflows of Black Americans changed perceptions of outgroup distance among native-born whites, reducing the barriers to the social integration of European immigrants. Predicting Black in-migration with a version of the shift-share instrument, we find that immigrants living in areas that received more Black migrants experienced higher assimilation along a range of outcomes, such as naturalization rates and intermarriages with native-born spouses. Evidence from the historical press and patterns of heterogeneity across immigrant nationalities provide additional support to the role of shifting perceptions of the white majority.


参考文献：

Fouka, V., Mazumder, S., & Tabellini, M. (2022). From immigrants to Americans: Race and assimilation during the Great Migration. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 811-842.



10、有条件的超强预测能力

 Conditional Superior Predictive Ability


本文提出了针对基准的一系列预测方法的条件优越预测能力(CSPA)测试。该测试本质上是功能性的：在原假设下，基准的条件预期损失不超过竞争对手，且在所有条件状态下一致。通过反转基准CSPA测试，我们获得了一致最优越方法的置信度集。计量经济学推论涉及测试具有一般序列依赖性的时间序列数据的条件矩不等式，我们使用基于新的强近似混合理论的统一非参数推理方法证明其渐近有效性。该方法的有用性在波动率和通货膨胀预测的经验应用中得到了证明。


This article proposes a test for the conditional superior predictive ability (CSPA) of a family of forecasting methods with respect to a benchmark. The test is functional in nature: under the null hypothesis, the benchmark’s conditional expected loss is no more than those of the competitors, uniformly across all conditioning states. By inverting the CSPA tests for a set of benchmarks, we obtain confidence sets for the uniformly most superior method. The econometric inference pertains to testing conditional moment inequalities for time series data with general serial dependence, and we justify its asymptotic validity using a uniform non-parametric inference method based on a new strong approximation theory for mixingales. The usefulness of the method is demonstrated in empirical applications on volatility and inflation forecasting.


参考文献：

Li, J., Liao, Z., & Quaedvlieg, R. (2022). Conditional superior predictive ability. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 843-875.



11、儿童营养的长期影响：来自学校午餐改革的证据

Long-term effects of childhood nutrition: Evidence from a school lunch reform


我们研究相关政策对儿童营养的长期影响。我们评估了这样一项政策，该政策在1959年至1969年期间向瑞典小学的所有学生免费提供营养丰富的学校午餐。我们估计该政策对儿童一生的经济、教育和健康的影响。我们的研究结果表明，学校午餐计划产生了可观的长期收益，在整个小学期间经历该政策的学生的终生收入提高了 3%。对于年龄较小的学生和来自贫困家庭的学生来说，该政策效果更大，这表明该计划减少了成年期的经济不平等。参与该政策还对学生的教育程度和健康程度产生了重大影响，这可以解释该政策对终生收入的很大一部分影响。


We study the long-term impact of a policy-driven change in childhood nutrition. For this purpose, we evaluate a program that rolled out nutritious school lunches free of charge to all pupils in Swedish primary schools between 1959 and 1969. We estimate the impact of the program on children’s economic, educational, and health outcomes throughout life. Our results show that the school lunch program generated substantial long-term benefits, where pupils exposed to the program during their entire primary school period have 3% higher lifetime income. The effect was greater for pupils that were exposed at earlier ages and for pupils from poor households, suggesting that the program reduced socioeconomic inequalities in adulthood. Exposure to the program also had substantial effects on educational attainment and health, which can explain a large part of the effect of the program on lifetime income.


参考文献：

Lundborg, P., Rooth, D. O., & Alex-Petersen, J. (2022). Long-term effects of childhood nutrition: Evidence from a school lunch reform. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 876-908.



12、总消费需求的广泛边际

The extensive margin of aggregate consumption demand 


美国非耐用品消费支出变化的大约一半原因是由于进入家庭消费篮子的产品变化（粗放边际）。篮子的变化是由家庭添加产品的速度波动所驱动的；去除产品的波动很小。这些特征反映出，为应对收入的增加，家庭选择了市场上已有的消费品。家庭的选择将财政转移支付对消费的影响扩大了30% 以上。家庭对产品的周期性购入还意味着，在我们数据涵盖的消费类别中，基于消费市场上所有可用品种的代表性家庭的通胀指标，低估了在大衰退期间的家庭通胀率的真实水平，低估值高达每年1%。


About half of the change in U.S. non-durable consumption expenditure is due to changes in the products entering households’ consumption basket (the extensive margin). Changes in the basket are driven by fluctuations in the rate at which households add products; removals fluctuate little. These patterns reflect that, in response to income increases, households adopt consumer products already available in the market. Household adoption amplifies the effects of fiscal transfers on consumption by more than 30%. Cyclical household adoption of products also implies that inflation measures based on a representative household consuming all varieties available in the market underestimate true household-level inflation by as much as 1% per year over the Great Recession in the consumption categories covered by our data.


参考文献：

Michelacci, C., Paciello, L., & Pozzi, A. (2022). The extensive margin of aggregate consumption demand. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 909-947.



13、合理性、可观察性和常识

Rationalizability, Observability, and Common Knowledge


我们研究了参与者的高阶不确定性对一般两人博弈中的行为可观察性的战略影响。具体而言，我们考虑了由于不确定是静态博弈还是完美信息博弈而产生的所有信念可能的空间。在这个空间中，我们描述了一个捕捉了理性和理性中的共同信念(RCBR)的行为含义，其中“理性”表明博弈是动态时，也是顺序的。我们表明，这种对应通常是单值的，并且它的结构支持合理化的稳健性改进，这通常具有非常显著的含义。例如，（1）在一类博弈中，既包括具有纯均衡的零和博弈，也包括具有唯一有效均衡的协调博弈，RCBR一般确保有效的均衡结果（归纳协调）；(2) 在还包括其他经典协调博弈家族的一类博弈中，RCBR一般选择 Stackelberg组合的成分（Stackelberg 选择）；(3) 如果参与者2的行为的不可观察是众所周知的（例如，因为众所周知参与者1会更早移动等），在包含上述所有RCBR的一类博弈中，参与者1一般选择静态博弈的均衡最有利（先发优势的普遍性）。


We study the strategic impact of players’ higher-order uncertainty over the observability of actions in general two-player games. More specifically, we consider the space of all belief hierarchies generated by the uncertainty over whether the game will be played as a static game or with perfect information. Over this space, we characterize the correspondence of a solution concept which captures the behavioural implications of Rationality and Common Belief in Rationality (RCBR), where “rationality” is understood as sequential whenever the game is dynamic. We show that such a correspondence is generically single-valued, and that its structure supports a robust refinement of rationalizability, which often has very sharp implications. For instance, (1) in a class of games which includes both zero-sum games with a pure equilibrium and coordination games with a unique efficient equilibrium, RCBR generically ensures efficient equilibrium outcomes (eductive coordination); (2) in a class of games which also includes other well-known families of coordination games, RCBR generically selects components of the Stackelberg profiles (Stackelberg selection); (3) if it is commonly known that player 2’s action is not observable (e.g. because 1 is commonly known to move earlier, etc.), in a class of games which includes all of the above RCBR generically selects the equilibrium of the static game most favourable to player 1 (pervasiveness of first-mover advantage).


参考文献：

Penta, A., & Zuazo-Garin, P. (2022). Rationalizability, Observability, and Common Knowledge. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 948-975.



14、热手谬误中的不确定性：检测随机伯努利序列的条纹替代方案

 Uncertainty in the Hot Hand Fallacy: Detecting Streaky Alternatives to Random Bernoulli Sequences


我们研究了一类伯努利序列集合随机性的排列检验，并将其应用于分析人类将连续成功的胜利过度视为正依赖的倾向——热手谬论（由于某件事发生了很多次，因此下次很可能再次发生）。特别地,我们基于将直接在k次连续成功之后的成功比例（或直接在k次连续失败之后的成功比例）与成功的总比例进行比较的数据，检验了排列测试是随机的原假设(即试验是独立同分布的)。我们刻画了这些检验统计量的渐近分布及其排列分布在随机、一组一般平稳过程和一类马尔可夫链替代下的特征，并由此得到它们的局部渐近幂。该检验用于评估四个受控篮球投篮实验提供的热手谬误。由于此实验需要更大的数据集以获得对篮球投篮随机偏差的测量。在一项我们能够获得数据的实验中，多个检验结果表明，一名射手的投篮模式与其随机偏差显著不一致——这提供了强有力的证据：表明篮球投篮并非始终对所有射手都是随机的。然而，我们发现在这个实验中反对随机的证据仅限于这个射手。我们的结果为实验的设计和检验提供了数学和统计基础，这些实验直接将随机偏差与人们对随机偏差的信念进行比较，从而实现了对热手谬误的直接检验。


We study a class of permutation tests of the randomness of a collection of Bernoulli sequences and their application to analyses of the human tendency to perceive streaks of consecutive successes as overly representative of positive dependence—the hot hand fallacy. In particular, we study permutation tests of the null hypothesis of randomness (i.e. that trials are i.i.d.) based on test statistics that compare the proportion of successes that directly follow k consecutive successes with either the overall proportion of successes or the proportion of successes that directly follow k consecutive failures. We characterize the asymptotic distributions of these test statistics and their permutation distributions under randomness, under a set of general stationary processes, and under a class of Markov chain alternatives, which allow us to derive their local asymptotic power. The results are applied to evaluate the empirical support for the hot hand fallacy provided by four controlled basketball shooting experiments. We establish that substantially larger data sets are required to derive an informative measurement of the deviation from randomness in basketball shooting. In one experiment, for which we were able to obtain data, multiple testing procedures reveal that one shooter exhibits a shooting pattern significantly inconsistent with randomness—supplying strong evidence that basketball shooting is not random for all shooters all of the time. However, we find that the evidence against randomness in this experiment is limited to this shooter. Our results provide a mathematical and statistical foundation for the design and validation of experiments that directly compare deviations from randomness with human beliefs about deviations from randomness and thereby constitute a direct test of the hot hand fallacy.


参考文献：

Ritzwoller, D. M., & Romano, J. P. (2022). Uncertainty in the hot hand fallacy: Detecting streaky alternatives to random bernoulli sequences. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 976-1007.



15、动态匹配的福利效应：实证分析

 Welfare Effects of Dynamic Matching: An Empirical Analysis


在不支付货币的情况下分配资源预计将会产生低效的分配。理论表明，在资源重复分配的情况下引入定量分配可以缓解这一问题，而由此带来的收益在大多数情况下需要实证检验。我们研究了密歇根自然资源部分配猎熊许可证的动态分配机制，发现它比静态分配机制更有效：该机制分配给参与者对他们来说具有较高价值的资源类型，同时也不将捕猎对其整体价值较高的参与者排挤出去。我们的实证分析也强调了参与者之间和资源分配之间的异质性对于动态分配机制效率的重要性。


Allocating resources without monetary payments is expected to yield inefficient allocations. Theory suggests that introducing rationing when resources are allocated repeatedly over time can mitigate this issue, while the magnitude of the resulting efficiency gains is an empirical question in most settings. We study a dynamic assignment mechanism used by the Michigan Department of Natural Resources to allocate bear hunting licenses and find that it yields a more efficient allocation than static mechanisms, allocating participants to types of resources for which they have a higher value without crowding out participants with a high overall value for hunting. Our empirical analysis also highlights the importance of heterogeneity across participants and across allocated resources for determining the efficiency of a dynamic allocation mechanism.


参考文献：

Verdier, V., & Reeling, C. (2022). Welfare effects of dynamic matching: An empirical analysis. The Review of Economic Studies, 89(2), 1008-1037.
